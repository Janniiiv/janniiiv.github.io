Titanic movie

Director: James Cameron

Genre: epic romance, drama, disaster

Year released: 1997

Awards: 11 Oscars
Fun fact: The sinking scene took over a month to film

The movie Titanic, tells the story of a real disaster: the sinking of the RMS Titanic, which happened in the year 1912.

The movie also contains a fictional love story between characters Jack and Rose as part of the plot.
